THE BIGGEST WEB TRENDS OF 2025

We’re already halfway through 2025, and it’s clear what digital design trends are leading the way so far.

In 2025, Designers have started creating sites as art, sites as interactive projects, and sites that simply exist for play and delight. This harkens back to the beginning of the web when designers found ways to show off new techniques or create websites for their own sake.
